Slushie inc is a slushie supply company that tracks consumption rate of its 
products by users, and supplies new orders when users run out of slushies.

Two windows.
Win 1: Slushie Vendor
- Supplies slushies to each house by clicking the "Supply" button
- Receives new slushie orders as tickets to supply or reject.
- New orders are automatically generated when an existing consumer has exhausted
  thier slushie or a new customer is added at the customer window
- Keeps Track of customer count, slushies per customer and total slushies 
  delivered
